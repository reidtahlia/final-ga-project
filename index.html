An ode to Peanut Butter

But first, which one are you?

Smooth

Smooth Peanut Butter Eaters Are:

  • More introverted
  • More risk-averse
  • More likely to avoid confrontation
  • More likely to arrive to work early
  • More likely to stay in on a Saturday night
  • More likely to be dissatisfied in relationship
  • Stressed out more easily
  • Fans of The Big Bang Theory

Crunchy

Crunchy Peanut Butter Eaters Are:

  • More extroverted
  • Bigger risk-takers
  • More likely to attract confrontation
  • More likely to arrive to work late
  • More likely to go out on a Saturday night
  • More satisfied in love life
  • More goal oriented
  • Fans of Game of Thrones

Poetry

In jars of glass, thou dost thyself adorn,
And wait to be consumed, so pure and true,
My palate doth rejoice from dusk till dawn,
Oh peanut butter, how I long for you.
